Audience

Target Audience

Our target audience is anyone between the ages of 20-70. Our focus is on outdoor enthusiasts that are looking for something adventurous to do or information about weather conditions in regards to these activities. They enjoy sports, hiking, rock climbing, boating, skiing, snowboarding, etc.

Personas

Adam Johnson



Adam is an IT director for Melaluca. Although the main office is in Idaho Falls, Adam works remotely from his home in Fish Haven. He is 24 years old and single. He enjoys spending time in nature. Some of his favorite activities include hiking, rock climbing and skiing.

Mary Carlson



Mary is 68 years old and widowed. She and her husband had 3 children, a son and two daughters. She is a retired nurse and loves spending her time traveling the world. Two of her kids ended up in Idaho so she visits quite often.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Mary is visiting her son is Preston, Idaho. She made plans to take the grandkids on a hike sometime this week but wants to see what the temperature will be so that they can plan accordingly. The little grandkids don't do well in cold weather.


Scenario 2: Mary's grandson, in Preston, has a football game at the local high school. As she prepared to leave the house, she noticed some dark clouds. She wants to check the weather to see if she needs to pack an umbrella.


Scenario 3: Adam woke up and was preparing to go to work. The temperature lately has been pretty inconsistent. He decided that he better check the weather forcast for the day.


Scenario 4: Adam heard that it might snow on his campling trip this weekend. He wants to know what kind of gear he will need to pack.


Scenario 5: Mary left Preston and was getting ready to head up to Soda Springs to visit her daughter. She wanted to know what outfit to wear for the day. She decided to check online for the weather forcast.
