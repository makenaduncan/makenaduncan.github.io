Audience

Target Audience

Our target audience is anyone between the ages of 20-70. Our focus is on outdoor enthusiasts that are looking for something adventurous to do or information about weather conditions in regards to these activities. They enjoy sports, hiking, rock climbing, boating, skiing, snowboarding, etc.

Personas

Adam Johnson

Adam is an IT director for Melaluca. He is 24 years old and single. He enjoys spending time in nature. Some of his favorite activities include hiking, rock climbing and skiing.

Mary Carlson

Mary is 68 years old and widowed. She and her husband had 3 children, a son and two daughters. She is a retired nurse and loves spending her time traveling the world. Two of her kids ended up in Idaho so she visits quite often.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Mary is visiting her son and daughter in Idaho Falls, Idaho. She made plans to take the grandkids to Jackson Hole sometime this week but wants to see what the temperature will be so that they can plan accordingly. The little grandkids don't do well in cold weather.

Scenario 2: Mary's grandson is has a football game at the local high school. As she prepared to leave the house, she noticed some dark clouds. She wants to check the weather to see if she needs to pack an umbrella.

Scenario 3: Adam woke up and was preparing to go to work. The temperature lately has been pretty inconsistent. He decided that he better check the weather forcast for the day.

Scenario 4: Adam heard that it might snow on his campling trip this weekend. He wants to know what kind of gear he will need to pack.

Scenario 5: Mary wanted to know what outfit to wear for the day. She was going to be out running errands for several hours. She got on the website to see what the low for the day was going to be.
