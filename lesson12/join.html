Membership Application

Welcome to the Greater Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce Membership Application page. We consider it an honor that your organization is considering being a Chamber Member. Please fill out the required fields on the Membership Application below. If at any time you have questions concerning membership, please contact Terra Thueson, our Membership Coordinator, at 208-523-1010 Opt. 6 or send an email to membership@idahofallschamber.com. To make Chamber Membership affordable for any size business, we base our dues on your employee count. Please note that 2 part time employees equal 1 full-time employee (FTE). Additionally, your total New Membership amount includes a $50 one-time account activation charge.
