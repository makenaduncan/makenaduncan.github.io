Our Purpose

The Greater Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce creates a foundation for its members by being the leading business resource for networking, advocacy, and education. We work together with businesses to create and protect a competitive advantage for business in the greater Idaho Falls area. The Chamber creates opportunities for your business through our many services and programs.
